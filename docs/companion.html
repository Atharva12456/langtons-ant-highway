Companion technical report

Atharva Jillhewar · revised 21 July 2026

Scope: this is a research ledger supporting the main paper. It does not prove or disprove the highway conjecture. The main paper is the submission document and controls if terminology differs.

The revised companion removes superseded reflection, Hamming-isolation, touch-graph-rank, and implementation-independence claims. It records the two separate remaining requirements: universal entrance into a bounded active regime and classification of all positive-growth periodic traces. It now includes the width–growth inequality, the six-mask and period–drift bounds at width four, the computer-assisted all-period width-four exclusion, its independent certificates, and the width-six local oscillator that prevents a naive extension.
